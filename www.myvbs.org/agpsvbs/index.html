Aatmik Ghar Prarthana Sangati

(AGPS)



The Brightest Week of Summer!

Imagine a world where curious kids become hands-on inventors who discover they're lovingly crafted by God. Spark creativity with interactive decoration and experiences.

Each day at AGPS VBS, kids travel through field-tested rotations that reinforce relevant Bible Points and immerse kids in new adventures. (small groups of up to 5 kids and one leader). Every station is designed to reinforce the daily Bible Point in a hands-on, interactive way that keeps everyone engaged.

At AGPS vacation Bible school, kids experience God’s Word in surprising and unforgettable ways! Each day, leaders reinforce one simple Bible truth—which makes it easy for kids to remember and apply to real life!

Daily Activities include:

Sing and Play Roar: Gear up for the day’s adventure by singing fun songs and meeting “Inventor” Ian!

Wild Bible Adventure: Experience each Bible story with all five senses.

Imagination Station: Create surprising and authentic faith discoveries with Sciency-Fun Gizmos.

Stampede Sports: Play high-energy games like Right Recipe Relay or Wacky Water Way!

KidVid Cinema: Watch films about real kids living out their faith in really cool ways.

Safari Celebration: A wrap up with music, drama, science experiments, and more!
